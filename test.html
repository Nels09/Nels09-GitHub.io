Dear Dad,

I am yet to see a man stronger than you, the level of strength you exude is surprising. I know you are a man and, it is said that men ought to bear pain as a show of strength but, your resilience is next to none. You are my role model, each time I look at you, I want to do more, I want to be better. You have seen me through a lot of hurdles in life, you have never complained. Saying thank you for all you do will never be enough appreciation. Remember when I went under the knife? You had gone under the knife few days before and, you were nursing your wound but, you had to go to work just to ensure you raise money for my bills. You were in pain, I could see it but, what mattered to you was my life.

You went hungry severally, you had sleepless nights, just so I could go to school, so I could become a better person, only God can reward you. Sometimes you want to give up but, because of your love for me and the family as a whole, you find the strength to carry on.

This tribute is for you, you are my hero, you are the best. This is for the world to see and know that I have the best father in the world. I love you now and always.
